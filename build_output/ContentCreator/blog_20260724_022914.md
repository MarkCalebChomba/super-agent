# Best free AI tools in 2026

**1. Google's AI-powered Writing Tool: Bard**

Google's AI-powered writing tool, Bard, is a game-changer for content creators. With Bard, you can generate high-quality content, including articles, social media posts, and even entire blog posts, in minutes. To use Bard, simply type a prompt, and the tool will generate a draft. You can then refine and edit the content to suit your needs.

**2. Canva's AI-powered Graphic Design Tool: Magic Write**

Canva's Magic Write is an AI-powered graphic design tool that helps you create stunning visual content, including infographics, social media graphics, and more. With Magic Write, you can simply type a description, and the tool will generate a design concept. You can then customize the design to suit your brand's style and preferences.

**3. Grammarly's AI-powered Grammar and Spell Checker: Grammarly**

Grammarly is a popular AI-powered writing tool that helps you improve your grammar, spelling, and punctuation. With Grammarly, you can write with confidence, knowing that your content is error-free and polished. Grammarly's AI-powered algorithm checks your writing for grammar, spelling, and punctuation errors, suggesting corrections and improvements along the way.

**4. Lumen5's AI-powered Video Creation Tool: Lumen5**

Lumen5 is an AI-powered video creation tool that helps you create stunning videos, including explainer videos, social media videos, and more. With Lumen5, you can simply upload your script, and the tool will generate a video, complete with animations, transitions, and music.

**5. Ahrefs' AI-powered SEO Tool: Ahrefs**

Ahrefs is a popular AI-powered SEO tool that helps you optimize your content for search engines. With Ahrefs, you can analyze your website's traffic, identify keywords, and track your rankings in real-time. Ahrefs' AI-powered algorithm also provides insights on how to improve your content's visibility and drive more traffic to your website.
