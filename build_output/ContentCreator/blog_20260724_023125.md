**1. Grammarly's AI-Powered Writing Assistant**

Grammarly is a popular writing tool that offers an AI-powered writing assistant for free. This tool helps you refine your writing by suggesting grammar, spelling, and punctuation corrections, as well as improving sentence structure and tone. With Grammarly, you can:

* Ensure error-free writing with its robust grammar and spell checker
* Enhance your writing style and tone with its advanced suggestions
* Get instant feedback on your writing to improve your content quality

For example, if you're writing an article and want to ensure that your headings are optimized for SEO, Grammarly's AI-powered writing assistant can help you suggest the most relevant keywords and phrases.

**2. Canva's AI-Driven Graphic Design Tool**

Canva is a popular graphic design platform that offers a range of free AI-driven design tools. With Canva, you can create professional-looking graphics, videos, and social media posts without any design expertise. Canva's AI-powered design tool can help you:

* Create custom graphics and logos with its extensive template library
* Design social media posts and ads with its AI-driven design suggestions
* Edit and enhance your photos with its advanced image editing tools

For instance, if you want to create a social media post with a custom graphic, Canva's AI-powered design tool can help you choose the perfect layout, font, and color scheme.

**3. Otter's AI-Powered Meeting Notes Tool**

Otter is an AI-powered meeting notes tool that allows you to record and transcribe meetings, interviews, and lectures. With Otter, you can:

* Record and transcribe meetings in real-time with its AI-powered transcription feature
* Identify key takeaways and action items with its advanced note-taking feature
* Share meeting notes with team members and stakeholders instantly

For example, if you're conducting a meeting with a client and want to ensure that you capture all the key points, Otter's AI-powered meeting notes tool can help you record and transcribe the meeting in real-time, saving you hours of time and effort.
