# Best free AI tools in 2026

**Unlock the Power of AI: Top 3 Free AI Tools in 2026**

As AI technology continues to advance at an unprecedented pace, businesses and individuals are increasingly looking for ways to harness its power without breaking the bank. Fortunately, there are numerous free AI tools available that can help you automate tasks, enhance creativity, and make data-driven decisions. In this article, we'll explore three of the best free AI tools in 2026 that can help you unlock the full potential of AI.

**1. Google Cloud AI Platform's AutoML**

Google Cloud AI Platform's AutoML is a free AI tool that allows you to build custom machine learning models without requiring extensive coding or data science expertise. With AutoML, you can train and deploy machine learning models in a matter of minutes, making it an ideal solution for small businesses and individuals who want to get started with AI quickly. For example, you can use AutoML to build a predictive model that forecasts sales based on historical data, or to create a chatbot that responds to customer inquiries.

**2. Adobe After Effects' AI-Powered Video Editing**

Adobe After Effects is a popular video editing software that has been enhanced with AI-powered capabilities in recent years. The free version of After Effects includes a range of AI-powered tools that can help you edit videos quickly and efficiently. For instance, the "Frame Removal" tool uses AI to automatically remove unwanted objects from your videos, while the "Content-Aware Fill" tool uses AI to fill in missing areas of your footage. With these tools, you can create professional-looking videos without requiring extensive video editing experience.

**3. Microsoft Azure's Text Analytics**

Microsoft Azure's Text Analytics is a free AI tool that allows you to analyze text data from various sources, including social media, email, and customer feedback. With Text Analytics, you can extract insights from unstructured text data, identify sentiment analysis, and even generate text summaries. For example, you can use Text Analytics to analyze customer feedback on social media and identify areas where your company can improve its products or services. You can also use Text Analytics to generate automated reports and summaries that help you make data-driven decisions.
