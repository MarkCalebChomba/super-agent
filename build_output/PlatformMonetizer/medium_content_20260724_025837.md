To build a successful AI agent, I first needed to identify a niche with high earning potential. After conducting extensive research, I settled on creating a chatbot that could assist small business owners with their social media management. I chose this niche because:

* Small businesses often struggle to manage their social media presence, leaving a gap in the market for AI-powered solutions.
